Who we are

We are an Urban Research and Development Lab, an interdisciplinary team of researchers, designers, and technologists curious about our cities. We research their complexities, innovate on top of their procceses, and challenge ourselves — and others — to think about them in new ways.

The world we are working towards

We are brought together by a shared desire to understand and improve our cities, by a passion for activating academic and local knowledge to make urban co-existence more hospitable, more human.

How we work

Our work spans three domains: research, innovation, and education. Our work comes to life through projects run for our partners, and in our experimental education and community space in Helsinki, the SPIN Hub.
