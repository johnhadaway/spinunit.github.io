SPIN Unit

We are an Urban Research and Development Lab, an interdisciplinary team of researchers, designers, and technologists curious about our cities. We research their complexities, innovate on top of their procceses, and challenge ourselves — and others — to think about them in new ways.

Space

The SPIN Hub is our experimental education and community space in Helsinki, located in the Punavuori district.
